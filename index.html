My experience in the CSE world!

I am always eager to learn new things, especially when it involves coding, In the summer of 2019, I was a lead TA in a pilot program at Michigan State University that lead incoming freshman(women) into an introductory to coding course through the College of Engineering. I am also a teaching assistant for an intro to python class at MSU as well. I absolutely love teaching students python. I know exactly how daunting coding can feel to some, mostly because I felt the same way when I first started and often still do because there are SO many different areas of python a person could learn. I believe TA'ing really helps me better understand coding in a different perspective by being able to think about solving problems in multiple different ways. It also continues to help build my understanding and knowledge of code.
